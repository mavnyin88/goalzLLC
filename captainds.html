About Captain D's:

Quality Seafood, Freshly Prepared

At Captain D’s Seafood Restaurant, we’re serious about serving great food—and great food begins with the highest quality ingredients. We go to great lengths to ensure that only the best fish, seafood, poultry, and vegetables wind up on your plate.

We season your meals to order, and grilled, broiled, and steamed items come straight from the stove to your plate. Sure, we’re known for fast service—but we never sacrifice quality for speed. We’re passionate about serving great tasting food, and we work hard to deliver on this promise.
